# Frequently Asked Questions

## Why did you choose infrastructure/DevOps over software development?
When I was searching for a job after graduation, I was focused on traditional development (C#, .NET, JavaScript). I didn't know much about cloud computing at the time. However, joining NorthBay Solutions caused a complete 180-degree shift in my technological mindset. I initially started in Big Data training, but quickly requested a move to DevOps. Once I experienced the power of automating infrastructure, microservices, and containerization using AWS, I fell in love with it and decided to dedicate my career to this domain.

## What kind of company culture are you looking for?
I prefer product-based companies or cultures where I can take ownership of the product. In project-based work, you often just build features and never see the end result or how customers interact with it. I want to work in a collaborative, team-spirited environment with a shared goal, where I can share ideas confidently and see them implemented.
